Cancellations and Early Departures

Cancellations must be received by 3 PM Mountain Standard Time at least 24 hours prior to scheduled arrival date or you may be subject to charges for one night’s stay at the rate you had reserved, using the credit card that guaranteed your reservation.

If you made your reservation through a third-party website, you must cancel your reservation through that website and obtain their cancellation number.

If you made your reservation through us, call us at 1-970-247-4042.

Early departures: The hotel charges for early departures.

Payments and Charges

Debit cards: Some debit cards treat authorizations as charges. Please contact your bank with any questions.

An invalid credit card will automatically cancel your reservation.

Credit card check-in procedure: At check-in, the credit card on file will be authorized for all room charges plus $150.00 to cover possible damage or loss.

Hours and Miscellaneous 

Check-in: 3 pm. ***Minimum check-in age is 21***

Check out: 11 am

Lobby Hours Summer: 7am to 10pm Winter: 7 am to 9pm

Free light continental breakfast: 7am to 10am daily.

Pets: We are not able to accommodate pets.

No smoking: Adventure Inn Durango is a non-smoking property. A $250 fee will be charged if smoking occurs in guest rooms.
