Local Activities

An outdoor adventure awaits your arrival…

What would you like to see or do while in Colorado? We’re here to help! Experience the rich history of Mesa Verde National monument. Skiing or an Alpine slide down the side of a mountain? Hiking, mountain biking or Animas river rafting; ever been zip-lining? A must is the Silverton & Durango Narrow Gauge train ride. Would you like to see the bands of the Milky Way and a million stars from a mountain top? We have all this and more in gorgeous Southwest Colorado!
