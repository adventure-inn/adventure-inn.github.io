Our Room Offerings

Pick a room that best suits your taste and budget

We are a non-smoking, no pets, ultra-clean facility. Most of our rooms are on the ground floor; please note that we do not have an elevator. Only one of our rooms is wheelchair accessible with handicap features; please call to determine availability.

Small Queen

Smaller standard room with 1 Queen bed – maximum occupancy 2 guests. Complimentary freshly-ground and brewed Starbucks coffee & assorted teas during office hours. Free light continental breakfast from 7am to 10am daily.

Room Features

Exceptional free Wi-Fi, large flat screen TVs with cable including HBO, heat/AC, microwave and mini-fridge, charge/docking station.
